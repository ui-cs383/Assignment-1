The Star System game allows the players to adequately familiarize themselves with a few different aspects of the game. The most notable of these was the Mission Phase, which is arguably one of the more important phases for the Rebel player (at least early game, when considering the Province/Galaxy renditions). And in the process of learning the Mission Phase, you will inevitably encounter character combat, either against other characters, squads (stacked military units), irate locals, or creatures.

Though not a lot of opportunities were presented for either player to detect one another, there were plenty of searches performed, which were often uneventful. Military action never amounted to much, seeing as how the Rebels had difficulties amassing any kind of power in so few rounds of play, and because only one star system was in use, space combat was not permitted, and the potential for movement was limited.

Outlined below is a step by step play through of all the phases for the Rebels and Imperials first turn; a single game turn.

Rebels Player

  • 1 Operation Phase

  • Movement Segment

    Rebel player starts from space, attempting to land on one of four available Environs. As the rebel spaceship entered the Environ, the Imperial player was able to detect the incoming ship via one of the up PDBs (Detection Table was referenced).

  • Enemy Reaction Segment

    Imperial player was unable to react, because in the Star System game, it is only possible to move from one Environ on a planet to another Environ on the same planet (only one planet in the start system has more than one Environ, which the Rebel player did not occupy).

  • Military Combat Segment

    Rebel player did not have military units present, so this segment was skipped.

  • 2 Search Phase

  • Imperial player attempted to search out and attack detected Rebel characters with military units and Imperial characters independently (Search Table was referenced).

  • 3 Mission Phase

  • Mission Assignment Segment

    Rebel player separated their three characters into two mission groups, acting in the same Environ.

  • Mission Action Segment

    Rebel player attempted to gain a new character and start a Rebel camp. In the process of drawing actions cards, irate locals initiated character combat (Irate Locals Chart & Character Combat Results Table referenced). After dealing with irate locals, the Rebel player successfully started a Rebel camp.

  • Bonus Draw Segment

    Rebel character bonuses allowed the Rebel player to successfully find/gain a new character, by drawing bonus cards.

Imperial Player

  • 4 Operation Phase

  • Movement Segment

    Imperial player moves military and Imperial characters around the star system, making use of their mobile military units and their lone starship respectively.

  • Enemy Reaction Segment

    Segment skipped.

  • Military Combat Segment

    Segment skipped.

  • 5 Search Phase

  • The Rebel player at this point is unable to detect the Imperial characters, and thus is unable to search for them, unless they are stacked with military units (which they were not). Segment skipped.

  • 6 Mission Phase

  • Mission Assignment Segment

    Imperial player separated their two characters into two mission groups, acting in separate Environs.

  • Mission Action Segment

    Imperial player attempted to gain a new character and assassinate a detected Rebel character. In the process, Rebel characters detected, performed a search (Search Table referenced) and ultimately found the Imperial assassin; character vs. character combat was initiated (Character Combat Results Table referenced), and Imperial character died. Remaining Imperial character gained a new ally.

  • Bonus Draw Segment

    There was no need to draw bonus cards. Segment skipped.

By the end of the fourth turn, the Rebel player had been completely eradicated, with all their characters either being killed or captured by the Imperials. Furthermore, only one instance of military combat was initiated when the Rebels were able to convert a militia unit via the Mission Phase; the fight was over before it began.
